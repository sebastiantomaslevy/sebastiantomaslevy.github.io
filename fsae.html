FSAE Fuel Tank Design

Role: Powertrain Team Member | Tools: SolidWorks, Ansys, TIG Welding

The Challenge

The 2025 FSAE regulations required a fuel capacity of X liters while fitting within a constrained chassis envelope. Previous designs suffered from fuel starvation during high-G cornering.

The Solution

I designed a custom aluminum fuel tank featuring:

  • Baffle System: Integrated internal baffling to minimize sloshing and maintain fuel pickup.
  • Material Selection: Switched to 5052-H32 Aluminum for superior weldability and fatigue resistance.
  • Validation: Performed fluid flow simulations (CFD) to verify flow rates at 1.5G lateral acceleration.

The Result

The final tank reduced weight by 15% compared to the previous year and successfully passed the technical inspection "tilt test" without leakage.
